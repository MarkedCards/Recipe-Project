Deep Fried Phone

Ingreidents

  • Phone
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Ginger
  • Peprika
  • Pepper
  • 1/4 tablespoon of chopped green onions
  • 1/4 tablespoon of diced onion
  • 1/4 tablespoon crushed garlic

Cooking Instructions

  1. Take the phone and brine it in a combination of salt, peprika and pepper for no less than 72 hours
  2. Saulte the onions garlic and ginger in the oil
  3. fry the phone until cracked and golden brown
  4. top the phone with the veg
